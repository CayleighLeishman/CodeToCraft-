Pine Ridge Mall

Pine Ridge Mall in Pocatello is a bustling shopping destination, offering a wide variety of stores, from popular retail chains to unique local boutiques. The mall features a spacious food court, a modern cinema, and a lively entertainment zone with arcade games and activities for all ages. With its convenient location and diverse shopping and entertainment options, Pine Ridge Mall is the perfect place for a day out with family and friends

Ross Park Swimming

The Pocatello Swimming Pool, located in the heart of Pocatello, Idaho, is a state-of-the-art aquatic facility that has been a cornerstone of the community since its grand opening in 2015. This modern swimming complex features an Olympic-sized pool, a diving well, and a dedicated children's splash area, making it a perfect destination for swimmers of all ages and skill levels.

Clean Museum

Welcome to the Clean Museum, where we explore the fascinating history and science of cleanliness and hygiene. Located in Pocatello, Idaho our interactive exhibits highlight the evolution of sanitation practices from ancient times to the present day. We offer engaging educational programs and community outreach to promote the importance of hygiene in public health. Discover the clean side of history with us and see how these practices have shaped our world.

Pocatello Restaruant

Welcome to El corporal Mexicano in Pocatello, where authentic Mexican flavors come to life. Our vibrant menu features traditional dishes, from sizzling fajitas to savory enchiladas, all made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Enjoy a festive atmosphere with colorful décor and live mariachi music. Come experience the rich culinary heritage of Mexico right here in Pocatello!
